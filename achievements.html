Hon Haji sheqow oo Hagajiyay Qolki Ay sexanayey Ardayda Haji Girls ee dhawan Gubtay+Dharka schoolka

Following the recent unprecedented devastating fire outbreak in Yussuf Haji Girls Secondary school form two’s dormitory that was investigatively concluded to be as a result of a power surge, I took the swiftest necessary action of renovating the dormitory brand new and distributing school items such as: uniforms, boxes and beddings to comfort and compensate for my dear students.

In this challenging economic times, school-parents in Ijara Constituency have no burden of paying any cent whether fees or other school costs.

I'm proud and happy that learners lost no minute of learning after the fire incident because of my office's urgent intervention.

Under my leadership, my assurance remains, no learner has to miss a lesson for fess and no parent to be burden.
